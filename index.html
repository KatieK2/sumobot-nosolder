Hardware

  • Sumobot Jr
  • LaserCut and 3D patterns from https://github.com/makenai/sumobot-jr
  • Other parts from https://github.com/makenai/sumobot-jr#other-parts-needed
  • Assembly video http://sumobotkit.com/
  • 6x #4 x 1/4" wood screws - 2 for each wheel and 2 for the ball caster. Wider threads are self tapping.
  • The 3rd wheel: commonly a ball caster or ball bearing / marble. Could be any wheel. Have to 3d print that ball holder, or go right for a ball caster.
  • Cut from wood. Acrylic is shiny, but prone to cracking. You can easily drill new mounting holes through wood.

Construction

  • You don't need to spend $$ prototyping. Use cardboard, coroplast (lawn signs), Popsicle sticks and duct tape.
  • Using a larger screwdriver.
  • Protect your work surface and add a bit of grip with cardboard or a rubber mat.
